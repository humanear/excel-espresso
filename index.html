Our Story

A Passion for the Perfect Shot

Excel Espresso was born out of a simple belief — that great coffee can change your whole day. Nestled in the heart of Sioux Falls, South Dakota, our espresso bar is a sanctuary for coffee lovers who refuse to settle for ordinary.

We source only the finest single-origin and blended beans from certified farms around the world, roasted to perfection and pulled with the precision of a true craft. Whether you stop in for a quick shot before work or linger over a long-form latte on a Saturday morning, we promise you an experience that goes far beyond the cup.

Come as a stranger. Leave as a regular.
